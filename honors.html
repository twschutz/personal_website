One Team Coding

1 Team Coding is a student organization at the University of Michigan founded in Fall 2019. Its founding principle is to expand student leadership and computer programming knowledge and interest through team collaboration on innovative software projects. Each semester, our team discusses various possible opportunities for projects which we could undertake which will have an impact on those around us while also developing our own programming skills and learning new languages. This past semester, I collaborated with a team of over 30 engineers to develop a web app called TutorMe using a MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React Native, NodeJS). TutorMe seeks to help connect University of Michigan students with peer tutors who can assist them with their coursework. We believe that this will provided a platform for which students can form study groups and get academic assistance for not only large, popular classes but also smaller classes for which finding study resources or tutors is often difficult. Throughout the course of this project, I have learned a lot about programming with a MERN stack and have had to persevere through the process of learning new languages while also meeting deadlines and developing new features for the app. I have also been forced to think more creatively not only to solve programming challenges as I encounter them but also to design the app in a user-friendly, intuitive, and appealing way.
